## Intro {#intro}

Proper gear makes all the difference on a day hike. This guide covers essential equipment for summer hiking in varied terrain, from coastal trails to alpine ridges.

## Summer Hiking Gear {#summer-gear}

Lightweight essentials for warm-weather day hikes:

### Essentials

- **Sun hat**: Wide-brimmed, breathable hat to protect from sun and heat
- **Hydration pack / water bottle**: At least 1.5â€“2L for summer day hikes
- **Lightweight hiking shoes**: Breathable shoes with good grip; consider quick-dry materials
- **Sunscreen & sunglasses**: SPF 30+ and UV-protective sunglasses to protect skin and eyes
- **Light layer & rain shell**: Lightweight wind/rain shell and a thin base layer for changing conditions
- **Small first-aid kit**: Plasters, antiseptic wipe, blister care, and any personal meds

### Tips

- Check weather forecast before heading out
- Start early to avoid midday heat
- Bring snacks with salt and protein (nuts, trail mix, energy bars)
- Always tell someone your route and expected return time
- Pack out all trash - leave no trace

### Navigation

- Download offline maps on your phone (AllTrails, Komoot)
- Carry a physical map and compass as backup
- Note key landmarks and trail markers
- Track your progress and time to estimate return
